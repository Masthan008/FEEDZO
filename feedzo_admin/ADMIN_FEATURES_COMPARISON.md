# Feedzo Admin Panel vs StackFood Admin Panel - Feature Comparison

## Date: April 9, 2026

---

## StackFood Admin Panel Features (Reference)

### 1. Business Settings
- Company & Business Rules (name, location, email, timezone, currency, business models, order notifications)
- Multiple Business Model (commission-based, subscription-based)
- Order Verification (unique verification code for delivery)
- Home Delivery & Takeaway options (enable/disable)
- Scheduled Delivery (enable/disable)
- Order Cancellation settings (who can cancel, cancellation reasons)
- Refund Settings (enable/disable, refund reasons)

### 2. Deliveryman Settings
- Tips for deliveryman (enable/disable)
- Show earnings in app (enable/disable)
- Deliveryman self-registration (enable/disable)
- Maximum assigned order limit
- Order cancellation permission
- Maximum cash in hand

### 3. Restaurant Settings
- Restaurant order cancellation permission
- Restaurant self-registration (app, web, admin)
- Add/Edit/Delete restaurants
- Restaurant approval workflow
- VAT/TAX settings
- Estimated delivery time
- Cuisine & Zone assignment

### 4. Customer Settings
- Customer wallet (enable/disable)
- Referral earning (enable/disable)
- Customer verification (enable/disable)
- Loyalty points (enable/disable)
- Wallet purchase & referral bonus settings
- Loyalty point credentials

### 5. Language & Theme
- Multiple language setup
- Enable/disable languages
- Default language selection
- Theme settings for user/customer app
- Multiple pre-integrated themes

### 6. Subscription Management
- Create restaurant subscription packages
- Enable/disable packages
- Set package features & order limits
- Filter subscriber list
- Free trial period

### 7. Email & Communication
- Email templates for different business events
- Enable/disable emails for admin, restaurants, customers, deliverymen
- Automatic email sending

### 8. Pages & Content Management
- Gallery (store business images, upload zip, download)
- Social media links
- Legal pages (terms, privacy, refund, shipping, cancellation)
- About us page (mission, vision)

### 9. Employee Management
- Employee roles (create roles, module permissions, edit/delete)
- Employee management (add employees, name, zone, role, phone, image, account info)
- Employee list, edit, delete

### 10. Restaurant Management
- Zone Setup (pinpoint location, add zones, edit, enable/disable, delivery charges)
- Cuisine Management (add, enable/disable, edit, delete)
- Restaurant management (add, edit, delete, search, filter, availability)
- Food Category Setup (categories, sub-categories, priority, bulk import/export)

### 11. Food Management
- Food Addons (extra cheese, soft drinks, etc., price, restaurant-specific)
- Food Settings (add food with variations, view reviews & ratings)
- POS Section (manual order billing, assign deliverymen)

### 12. Order Management
- Order Details (scheduled, pending, accepted, processing, food on way, delivered, payment failed)
- Subscription Orders
- Order Refunds
- Dispatch Management (view ongoing orders, assign deliverymen manually)

### 13. Deliveryman Management
- Add new deliveryman
- View deliveryman list
- Accept/deny joining & incentive requests
- Give bonus (freelance & salary-based)
- View customer reviews
- Shift Setup (working hours)
- Vehicles Category Setup (type, charges, coverage area)

### 14. Promotion Management
- Campaigns (basic for all restaurants, food campaigns for specific food/zone/restaurant/category)
- Banners (add banners, zone selection, banner type, food selection)
- Coupons (coupon types, restaurant selection, generate codes, minimum discount)

### 15. Notification & Communication
- Push Notification (customized to customers, restaurants, deliverymen)
- Chatting & Contact Messages (connect with customers, deliverymen, restaurants)

### 16. Customer Management
- View, edit, delete customer information
- Contact info, total delivered orders, active/inactive status
- View wallet & loyalty balance
- Subscribed Emails (newsletter subscribers)

### 17. Wallet & Loyalty
- Add funds to customers' wallets
- Set references
- Loyalty Point management
- Referral system

### 18. Transaction Management
- Collect Cash (from restaurants/deliverymen via cash, card, bank transfer)
- Restaurant Withdraws (manage withdrawal transactions, set withdrawal methods)
- Deliveryman Payments (define payment methods, search deliverymen by name)

### 19. Reports & Analytics
- Dashboard (zone-wise & date-wise order statistics, earning graph, user statistics pie chart)
- Transaction & Expense Report (search, track, monitor, analyze)
- Food & Customer Report (food reports, customer wallet reports)
- Restaurant & Order Report (restaurant order statistics, subscription reports, regular orders, campaign orders)

### 20. 3rd Party & Configuration
- Payment methods
- SMS module
- Mail config
- Recaptcha
- Firebase notifications
- Login pages

### 21. App & Web Settings
- Minimum app version for user, restaurant, deliveryman apps
- Mandatory updates on Android and iOS
- App download URL
- Landing Page Settings (customize content without coding)

### 22. Database
- Clean Database (clear system database by features)
